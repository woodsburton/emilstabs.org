(This is taken straight from Brian's submission, however
I noticed this...the tune is based on the E Pentatonic Scale,
more accurately, the E Pentatonic Blues Scale, which adds a
flatted 5th...the "blue" note.  In this case, that note works
out to be Bb.  If you use the chord I posted here, you get a
Bb Major Triad, almost as if they are paying "homage" to the
Bb, or so it would appear to me...anywho, when you play it,
it sounds right on).


***Note*** I found it more comfortable to play the little Minor
3rd/Major 3rd fill up on the 5 & 6th frets of D and the 7th fret
of A.
